cetus is the earliest open world a player discovers in their playthrough, it rotates between day and night with each state giving diffrent rewards

day has more abundant yet less rare rewards as well as cetus having less danagerous enemies. day bounties can be done to get several resources including the gara warframe and her equipment

night is more dangerous as eidolons walk free, eidolon hunts become avilable which is crucial for progression as well as certain wildlife only being avilable at night instead of day. the revenant warframe and his equipment collected at night as well
